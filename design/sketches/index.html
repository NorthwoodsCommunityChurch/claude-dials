System notes

Status thresholds: green < 60 % · gold 60–85 % · coral > 85 %. Light blue (#009CDE) is interactive-only — never a status color. Surfaces are warm-black tiers from brand black #2D2926 (no pure black). Motion: snap — rings animate 0.18 s, countdowns hard-tick, nothing bounces. Fonts: Myriad Pro Regular / Semibold / Black, tabular numerals for all data.
